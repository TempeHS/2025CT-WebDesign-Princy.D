Modifying the Wii

The equipment you will need in order to modify your wii is an

  • SD card,
  • a wii (obviously)
  • and a computer. androids also work for this.

Alright, the first thing you want to do is go grab your MAC address for it. It is located in your console information, a catagory gotten from the Internet category. Memorise it or photo it and progress to https://wilbrand.donut.eu.org/ On the website, type your MAC address into there, check that the HackMii installer is packaged into there, and cut either wire. It does not matter. Take that zip file, and just copy everything and plug it back into the console. Turn it on, and check your Wii message board, located at the bottom right. If there is no green letter, check the day tomorrow, or the one to your right. If it doesn't show, do the process again, or troubleshoot your SD card. Click the mailbox and you will be sent to the HackMii void.

If you see this image, you should be doing everything right. You are also almost done with modding your console.


Finishing the installation and suggestions of other applications

After a few seconds, you should be given a prompt to continue. Press 1, and you should be in this sort of interactive console menu. Press continue, then install the homebrew channel. After that is installed, press back and then install HackMii. Once you've done that, you have modded your Wii, and you can safely exit. The homebrew menu should be on your Wii menu now.
